Virginia CTE Skills Demonstration

This skills-demonstration template establishes the repeatable FCA Academy model for VDOE-aligned CTE lessons: safety briefing, correct tool/material use, quality checkpoints, documentation, and student reflection.

Demonstration Checklist

  1. Review PPE, tool condition, lab boundaries, supervision ratio, and stop-work signals.
  2. Model the task slowly, naming measurements, tolerances, codes, and inspection criteria.
  3. Show the expected artifact, common defects, and correction process.
  4. Require learners to explain the safety and quality standard before hands-on work begins.
  5. Capture instructor notes for SCR evidence and improvement feedback.

Evidence Standard

Learners submit photo, drawing, document, measurement, or reflection evidence as directed by the instructor. Evidence must map to the module rubric and remain auditable for CTE review.
