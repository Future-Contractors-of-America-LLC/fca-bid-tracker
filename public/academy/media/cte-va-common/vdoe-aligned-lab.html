Virginia CTE Lab Packet

This lab packet is the common FCA Academy evidence model for Virginia Architecture & Construction CTE courses. Instructors adapt the hands-on task to the local lab, equipment, and official VDOE course framework.

Lab Evidence Requirements

  1. Safety briefing and PPE/tool check completed before work begins.
  2. Student artifact produced under instructor supervision.
  3. Photo, drawing, file, measurement, checklist, or reflection uploaded to the learner record.
  4. Instructor rubric completed against safety, technical accuracy, documentation, communication, and professionalism.
  5. HQWBL connection recorded when the lab is tied to employer, community, simulation, or school-based enterprise work.

Completion Gate

A lab is complete only when the instructor verifies the practical artifact, knowledge check, safety evidence, and Student Competency Record alignment in FCA Academy.
