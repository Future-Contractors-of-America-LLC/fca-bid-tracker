Virginia Architecture & Construction CTE Lecture

This FCA Academy lecture template supports Virginia CTE Architecture & Construction courses with a consistent instructor-led structure: official course framework, safety expectations, technical vocabulary, demonstration, learner evidence, and student competency record review.

Lecture Flow

  1. Confirm the official VDOE course page, prerequisite posture, grade level, duration, and local instructor requirements.
  2. Introduce the module objective, workplace relevance, and required safety controls.
  3. Teach the technical concept using drawings, specifications, tools, materials, codes, or project documentation.
  4. Connect the concept to Workplace Readiness Skills, SkillsUSA/CTSO behaviors, and high-quality work-based learning evidence.
  5. Prepare learners for the supervised lab, artifact upload, knowledge check, and instructor verification.

Instructor Verification

Completion requires a knowledge check, a practical artifact, safety sign-off where applicable, and instructor review against the Student Competency Record. FCA Academy stores the evidence trail; the instructor remains the authority of record.
