Future Contractors of America

Company and product overview

Future Contractors of America (FCA) is not in stealth mode. We are building an AI-powered operating system for construction that helps contractor teams move from opportunity to delivery with real workflows for bids, estimates, proposals, projects, files, billing, support, Academy training, and Auricrux guidance.

What FCA is

FCA connects leads, bids, projects, plan rooms, field operations, finance, billing, customer communication, and Academy training in one contractor workspace — with Auricrux guiding what to do next.

  • SaaS execution for contractor operations
  • FCA Academy for workforce training pathways
  • Customer portal and communications
  • Auricrux operating intelligence embedded across the platform
