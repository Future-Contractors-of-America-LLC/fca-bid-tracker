Company and product overview

Future Contractors of America (FCA) is not in stealth mode. We build an AI-native contractor platform meant to carry a team from opportunity through estimate, award, delivery, billing, closeout, warranty, and workforce training — with Auricrux able to guide the work or take the next action inside that same workspace.

What FCA is today

A live contractor workspace connecting leads, bids, projects, field operations, finance, billing, customer communication, and Academy training — with Auricrux available to recommend or execute the next step.

  • Live SaaS workspace spanning bid-to-bill and job delivery routes
  • FCA Academy pathways for workforce training (1,245 published programs in catalog)
  • Customer portal and in-app communications
  • Auricrux construction intelligence embedded across the platform
  • Continuous model training and promotion through our Auricrux control plane
