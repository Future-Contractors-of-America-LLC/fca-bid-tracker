Colonial Heights Public Schools × Future Contractors of America LLC — FCA Academy v1.0

Agreement text (Sections 1–13)

1. Purpose

This Data Privacy Agreement ("DPA") governs the collection, use, storage, and disclosure of student data in connection with FCA Academy, used by Colonial Heights Public Schools ("CHPS"). This DPA is entered into pursuant to FERPA, COPPA, SOPIPA (Va. Code § 22.1-287.02), VCDPA, PPRA, and SPPO guidance.

2. Definitions

Student Data includes course enrollment, lesson completion, quiz scores, progress state, and access timestamps. PII is defined under FERPA. FCA acts as a school official with legitimate educational interest.

FCA Academy does NOT collect: student legal name, DOB, SSN, address, phone, email, parent info, race/ethnicity/religion/disability, behavioral/biometric/geolocation data, device fingerprints, or any data not necessary for educational delivery.

4. Use Limitations

FCA uses Student Data solely to provide FCA Academy to CHPS; does not sell Student Data; does not use it for advertising or profiling; does not disclose except as required by law or authorized by CHPS; does not combine with other sources for non-educational purposes.

5. FERPA School Official Designation

CHPS designates FCA as a school official under FERPA 34 C.F.R. § 99.31(a)(1). FCA complies with FERPA, uses data only for disclosed purposes, does not re-disclose without authorization, and supports parent/guardian access requests through CHPS.

6. COPPA Compliance

Accounts are pre-provisioned; students do not self-register or submit PII. CHPS provides school consent under COPPA 16 C.F.R. § 312.5(b)(1) by executing this DPA.

7. Security Safeguards

Session-based auth with HttpOnly/Secure/SameSite=Strict cookies; 8-hour TTL and 30-minute student idle timeout; TLS 1.2+; AES-256 at rest; RBAC; HTTP 401 on unauthenticated protected routes; auth audit logging. Students are provisioned by administrators only.

8. Breach Notification

FCA notifies CHPS within 72 hours of discovery; written report within 7 days; cooperates on parent notification per Virginia law; no public statements without CHPS approval.

9. Data Retention and Deletion

Data retained for the active school year. Upon termination or CHPS request, deletion within 30 days with written confirmation; backups purged within 60 days.

10. Parental Rights

CHPS may request copies, correction, or deletion on behalf of parents; FCA fulfills within 10 business days. Parent requests flow through CHPS.

12. Term and Termination

Effective from countersignature through June 30 of the active school year. Either party may terminate with 30 days notice. CHPS may terminate immediately for material breach.

13. Governing Law

Commonwealth of Virginia; disputes in Colonial Heights, Virginia courts.
