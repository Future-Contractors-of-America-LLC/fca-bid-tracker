# FCA Contractor Command Qualification Packet

## Objective
Strengthen the flagship product spine at the first revenue-critical gate: convert raw lead activity into a qualified opportunity that can be priced, routed, and serviced without founder intervention.

## Product spine position
1. Lead and opportunity intake
2. Qualification command surface
3. File and evidence handling
4. Bid and estimate workflow
5. Client portal continuity
6. Audit trail and guided Auricrux assistance

## Qualification command decisions
The bid workspace now carries a qualification command surface for each package so FCA can make the next move from inside the operating shell instead of external notes.

Required qualification fields:
- qualification score
- qualification status
- budget fit
- scope fit
- jurisdiction posture
- travel posture
- evidence packet state
- next gate

## Operator outcomes
- confirm whether an opportunity is worth estimator time
- capture whether budget and scope are real
- record whether jurisdiction and travel requirements fit FCA coverage
- preserve a qualification packet before routing to estimate
- expose the next gate directly in the bid workspace

## Founder-hands-off impact
This reduces founder dependence by moving the qualify-or-stop decision into a repeatable command surface with persisted state, visible next gate, and automation/commercial log continuity.

## Immediate next enforcement step
Bind uploaded files and customer evidence directly to the qualification packet so the estimate handoff can prove why an opportunity was advanced.
