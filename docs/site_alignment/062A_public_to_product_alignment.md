# 062A Public-to-Product Alignment

## Required public promises
- FCA Contractor Command is reachable through a real authenticated workspace
- FCA Academy is reachable as a real integrated learning surface
- Auricrux is available across workflow explanation, recommendation, and execution

## Required public route mapping
- `/platform` must preview the actual portal categories already in repo
- `/pricing` must map commercial plans to real reachable surfaces
- `/login` must promise entry into real SaaS, LMS, and Auricrux areas
- `/contact`, `/warranty`, and `/referrals` must reinforce lifecycle continuity rather than stand alone

## Anti-drift rule
Public marketing language may not describe slices that cannot be reached from login, and it may not omit major slices that do already exist.
