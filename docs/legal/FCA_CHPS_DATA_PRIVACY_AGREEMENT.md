## 1. Purpose

This Data Privacy Agreement ("DPA") governs the collection, use, storage, and disclosure of student data in connection with FCA Academy, an online educational platform operated by Future Contractors of America LLC ("FCA", "Provider"), used by Colonial Heights Public Schools ("CHPS", "LEA", "District").

This DPA is entered into pursuant to:
- Family Educational Rights and Privacy Act (FERPA), 20 U.S.C. § 1232g
- Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA), 15 U.S.C. §§ 6501–6506
- Virginia Student Online Personal Information Protection Act (SOPIPA), Va. Code § 22.1-287.02
- Virginia Consumer Data Protection Act (VCDPA), Va. Code § 59.1-571 et seq.
- Protection of Pupil Rights Amendment (PPRA), 20 U.S.C. § 1232h
- U.S. Department of Education Student Privacy Policy Office (SPPO) guidance

---

## 2. Definitions

- **Student Data**: Any data or metadata generated by, collected from, or pertaining to a student in connection with their use of FCA Academy, including but not limited to: course enrollment, lesson completion, quiz scores, progress state, and access timestamps.
- **PII (Personally Identifiable Information)**: As defined by FERPA — data that can be used to distinguish or trace an individual's identity, directly or indirectly.
- **Education Records**: Records directly related to a student, maintained by the school or a party acting on behalf of the school, as defined under FERPA.
- **Authorized User**: A student, teacher, or administrator who has been granted access to FCA Academy by CHPS.
- **School Official**: FCA, acting in a capacity consistent with a legitimate educational interest under FERPA.

---

## 3. Scope of Data Collection

FCA Academy collects **only the following data** from student accounts:

| Data Element | Purpose | Retention |
|---|---|---|
| Non-identifying username (e.g., `student-042`) | Account login | End of school year |
| Role (`student`, `instructor`, `admin`) | Access control | End of school year |
| Enrolled course identifier | Progress tracking | End of school year |
| Lesson completion status | Educational progress | End of school year |
| Quiz/assessment scores | Educational measurement | End of school year |
| Access timestamps (hashed, no IP) | Security audit log | 90 days rolling |

**FCA Academy does NOT collect:**
- Student legal name, date of birth, or age
- Social Security Number or government ID
- Home address, phone number, or email address
- Parent/guardian information
- Race, ethnicity, religion, or disability status
- Behavioral data, biometric data, or geolocation
- Device fingerprints or persistent identifiers
- Any information not directly necessary for educational delivery

---

## 4. Use Limitations

FCA shall:
- Use Student Data **solely** for the purpose of providing and improving FCA Academy educational services to CHPS.
- **Not sell** Student Data to any third party.
- **Not use** Student Data for targeted advertising, behavioral profiling, or commercial purposes.
- **Not disclose** Student Data to any third party except as required by law or as authorized in writing by CHPS.
- **Not combine** Student Data with data from other sources for any non-educational purpose.

---

## 5. FERPA School Official Designation

CHPS hereby designates FCA as a **school official** with a legitimate educational interest, as permitted under FERPA 34 C.F.R. § 99.31(a)(1). FCA agrees to:
- Comply with all FERPA requirements applicable to school officials.
- Use Student Data only for the purpose for which it was disclosed.
- Not re-disclose Student Data without CHPS authorization.
- Support CHPS in responding to parent/guardian requests for access, correction, or deletion of Student Data.

---

## 6. COPPA Compliance

FCA Academy is designed to avoid collection of PII from children under 13. For the Year 1 pilot:
- All student accounts are pre-provisioned by FCA or CHPS administrators.
- Students do not register themselves or submit any personal information.
- No email address, name, or contact information is required or accepted.
- CHPS, as the operator of record under the "school consent" exception (COPPA, 16 C.F.R. § 312.5(b)(1)), provides consent on behalf of parents by entering into this DPA and provisioning student access.

---

## 7. Security Safeguards

FCA shall implement and maintain the following technical and organizational safeguards:

### Technical Controls
- **Authentication**: Session-based login with `HttpOnly`, `Secure`, `SameSite=Strict` cookies. No ambient or automatic login.
- **Session Management**: Maximum 8-hour session TTL; 30-minute idle timeout for student accounts.
- **Transport Security**: TLS 1.2+ enforced on all endpoints. No plaintext data transmission.
- **Data at Rest**: Student Data encrypted at rest in Azure Table Storage (AES-256).
- **Access Control**: Role-based access control (RBAC) — students can only access their own progress; instructors can view their cohort; admins have full read access with audit log.
- **No Seeded Fallback**: Protected routes return HTTP 401 for unauthenticated requests. No automatic login or session fabrication.
- **Audit Logging**: All authentication events (login, logout, session expiry, failed attempts) logged with timestamp, role, and hashed session identifier.

### Organizational Controls
- Student accounts are provisioned only by authorized FCA or CHPS administrators.
- Access codes are distributed by teachers — students never self-register.
- Annual security review and penetration testing planned before Year 2 renewal.
- Employee access to Student Data limited to personnel with a direct need.

---

## 8. Breach Notification

In the event of a confirmed or suspected breach of Student Data, FCA shall:
1. Notify CHPS IT Director and Privacy Officer **within 72 hours** of discovery.
2. Provide a written incident report within **7 days** including: nature of breach, data elements affected, estimated number of students affected, immediate containment actions taken, and remediation plan.
3. Cooperate with CHPS in notifying affected parents/guardians as required by Va. Code § 22.1-287.02 and Virginia breach notification law (Va. Code § 18.2-186.6).
4. Not make public statements about the breach without CHPS approval.

---

## 9. Data Retention and Deletion

- Student Data is retained for the duration of the active school year.
- Upon expiration of this DPA or written request from CHPS, FCA will **delete all Student Data within 30 days** and provide written confirmation.
- Backup copies will be purged within 60 days of the deletion request.
- Audit logs are retained for 90 days then automatically purged.

---

## 10. Parental Rights

In support of CHPS's FERPA obligations:
- CHPS retains the right to request a copy of any Student Data held by FCA at any time.
- CHPS may request correction or deletion of Student Data on behalf of a parent/guardian.
- FCA will fulfill such requests within **10 business days**.
- FCA does not directly interact with parents/guardians regarding Student Data — all such requests flow through CHPS.

---

## 11. Subprocessors

FCA uses the following subprocessors in connection with Student Data:

| Subprocessor | Purpose | Data Shared | Location |
|---|---|---|---|
| Microsoft Azure (Table Storage) | Student progress data storage | Progress records only | US East (Virginia) |
| Microsoft Azure (Static Web Apps) | Application hosting | No student data | US East |

No additional subprocessors will be added without 30-day prior written notice to CHPS.

---

## 12. Term and Termination

- This DPA is effective from the date of countersignature through June 30 of the active school year.
- Either party may terminate with 30 days written notice.
- Upon termination, FCA will complete data deletion per Section 9.
- CHPS may terminate immediately upon written notice if FCA materially breaches this DPA.
