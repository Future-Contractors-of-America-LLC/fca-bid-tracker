# Accessibility Statement

**Future Contractors of America LLC**  
**Version:** 1.0  
**Effective Date:** June 19, 2026  

> **Prepared for legal review — not legal advice.** Publish at https://futurecontractorsofamerica.com/accessibility after counsel approval.

---

## 1. Commitment

FCA is committed to making the Services reasonably accessible to users with disabilities. We aim to conform to the **Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA** for web-based surfaces over time.

---

## 2. Measures

We pursue accessibility through:

- Semantic HTML and ARIA labels where appropriate
- Keyboard navigability for core workflows
- Sufficient color contrast in default themes
- Text alternatives for meaningful images
- Accessibility considerations in design system updates
- Vendor accessibility review for third-party components

---

## 3. Known Limitations

Some legacy screens, PDF exports, third-party embeds, and mobile-specific views may not yet meet all WCAG 2.1 AA criteria. We maintain a remediation backlog prioritized by user impact.

---

## 4. VPAT / ACR

A **Voluntary Product Accessibility Template (VPAT)** or Accessibility Conformance Report is available to enterprise customers upon request at legal@futurecontractorsofamerica.com.

---

## 5. Feedback and Accommodation

If you encounter accessibility barriers:

**Email:** accessibility@futurecontractorsofamerica.com (or info@futurecontractorsofamerica.com)  
**Response target:** Five (5) business days for acknowledgment

We will work with you to provide reasonable accommodations or alternative access where feasible.

---

## 6. Standards and Laws

This statement supports compliance efforts under:

- Americans with Disabilities Act (ADA) — Title III where applicable to B2B SaaS
- Section 508 (U.S. federal procurement when applicable)
- EN 301 549 (EU public sector procurement when applicable)

---

## 7. Continuous Improvement

Accessibility is reviewed during major releases. Enterprise customers may report issues through their support channel.
