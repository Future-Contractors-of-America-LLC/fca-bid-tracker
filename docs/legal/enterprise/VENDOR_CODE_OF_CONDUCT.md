# Vendor Code of Conduct

**Future Contractors of America LLC**  
**Version:** 1.0  
**Effective Date:** June 19, 2026  

> **Prepared for legal review — not legal advice.** Incorporate into vendor/supplier agreements.

---

## 1. Application

This Vendor Code of Conduct ("**Code**") applies to suppliers, subprocessors, contractors, and partners providing goods or services to Future Contractors of America LLC ("**FCA**").

---

## 2. Legal Compliance

Vendors comply with all applicable laws, including labor, environmental, anti-corruption, export, and data protection laws.

---

## 3. Labor and Human Rights

- No forced, indentured, or child labor
- Fair wages and working hours per local law
- Non-discrimination and harassment-free workplace
- Freedom of association where legally permitted

---

## 4. Health and Safety

Vendors maintain safe working conditions and comply with applicable occupational health regulations.

---

## 5. Environmental

Vendors minimize environmental impact and comply with environmental laws. Prefer sustainable practices where commercially reasonable.

---

## 6. Data Protection and Security

Vendors processing personal data or FCA confidential information must:

- Implement appropriate security controls
- Process data only per FCA instructions and contracts
- Notify FCA of breaches without undue delay
- Flow down obligations to their subcontractors

---

## 7. Anti-Corruption

Vendors comply with the [Anti-Bribery and Corruption Policy](./ANTI_BRIBERY_AND_CORRUPTION_POLICY.md) and do not offer improper payments on FCA's behalf.

---

## 8. Intellectual Property

Vendors respect IP rights and assign or license deliverables per work-for-hire agreements.

---

## 9. Conflicts of Interest

Vendors disclose conflicts that could affect impartiality.

---

## 10. Monitoring

FCA may audit compliance with reasonable notice. Material breaches may result in termination.
