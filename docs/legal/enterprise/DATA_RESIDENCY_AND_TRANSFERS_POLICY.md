# Data Residency and Transfers Policy

**Future Contractors of America LLC**  
**Version:** 1.0  
**Effective Date:** June 19, 2026  

> **Prepared for legal review — not legal advice.**

---

## 1. Purpose

This policy describes where Customer Data is stored and processed, and the safeguards for international transfers.

---

## 2. Default Data Location

Unless otherwise agreed in an Order Form:

| Data type | Default region |
|-----------|----------------|
| Production Customer Data | **United States** (Microsoft Azure U.S. regions) |
| Backups | Same region as primary |
| Support tickets | United States |

---

## 3. Regional Options (Enterprise)

Enterprise Order Forms may specify:

- **US-only** processing (default)
- **EU** processing (when commercially available — requires separate Order Form and may incur additional fees)

Contact legal@futurecontractorsofamerica.com for data residency requirements during procurement.

---

## 4. International Transfers

### 4.1 Transfers from EEA/UK

When Personal Data is transferred from the European Economic Area or United Kingdom to the United States or other non-adequate countries, FCA relies on:

- **EU Standard Contractual Clauses (2021/914)** per the [DPA](./DATA_PROCESSING_AGREEMENT.md)
- **UK International Data Transfer Addendum** where applicable
- Supplementary technical measures: encryption in transit and at rest, access controls, pseudonymization where feasible

### 4.2 Transfer Impact Assessments

FCA monitors legal developments affecting transfers and updates mechanisms as required. Enterprise customers may request transfer impact assessment summaries under NDA.

---

## 5. Subprocessor Locations

Subprocessor regions are listed in the [Subprocessor List](./SUBPROCESSOR_LIST.md). FCA requires subprocessors to process data only in approved regions.

---

## 6. Data Localization Requests

Government or regulated-industry customers requiring data to remain in specific jurisdictions must document requirements during sales. FCA will assess feasibility before contract execution.

---

## 7. Onward Transfer

FCA does not sell Customer Data. Transfers to subprocessors are limited to providing the Services per the DPA.
