# Anti-Bribery and Corruption Policy

**Future Contractors of America LLC**  
**Version:** 1.0  
**Effective Date:** June 19, 2026  

> **Prepared for legal review — not legal advice.** Internal policy; summary available to enterprise procurement upon request.

---

## 1. Policy Statement

FCA prohibits bribery, kickbacks, and corrupt payments in all business dealings. All personnel and agents must comply with the U.S. Foreign Corrupt Practices Act (FCPA), UK Bribery Act 2010 (where applicable), and local anti-corruption laws.

---

## 2. Prohibited Conduct

- Bribes or improper payments to government officials, customers, or third parties to obtain or retain business
- Facilitation payments except where legally permitted and pre-approved by compliance
- Kickbacks, secret commissions, or undisclosed benefits
- False or incomplete books and records related to payments
- Use of third parties to channel improper payments

---

## 3. Gifts and Hospitality

Modest gifts and business hospitality are permitted only if:

- Legal in all relevant jurisdictions
- Reasonable in value and frequency
- Transparent and recorded
- Not intended to influence a business decision improperly

Pre-approval required for gifts or hospitality to government officials.

---

## 4. Third Parties

Agents, distributors, consultants, and subcontractors engaged for sales or government-facing activities undergo risk-based due diligence. Contracts include anti-corruption representations.

---

## 5. Government Contracting

Additional restrictions apply to U.S. federal, state, and foreign government sales. Personnel receive targeted training before engaging in government procurement.

---

## 6. Books and Records

All transactions are recorded accurately. No off-book accounts or slush funds.

---

## 7. Reporting

Report suspected violations to legal@futurecontractorsofamerica.com. Retaliation against good-faith reporters is prohibited.

---

## 8. Enforcement

Violations may result in termination and referral to law enforcement.

---

## 9. Training

Personnel with sales, procurement, or government-facing roles receive anti-corruption training at onboarding and periodically thereafter.
