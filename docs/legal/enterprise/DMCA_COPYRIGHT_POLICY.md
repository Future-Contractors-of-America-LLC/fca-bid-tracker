## 3. Filing a Notice

To report copyright infringement, send a written notice including:

1. Physical or electronic signature of copyright owner or authorized agent
2. Identification of copyrighted work claimed infringed
3. Identification of infringing material and its location (URL, workspace ID)
4. Your contact information
5. Good-faith belief statement that use is not authorized
6. Statement under penalty of perjury that information is accurate and you are authorized

---

## 4. Counter-Notification

If material was removed in error, send counter-notification per 17 U.S.C. § 512(g) including:

1. Signature
2. Identification of removed material and prior location
3. Consent to jurisdiction of federal court in your district (or Virginia if outside U.S.)
4. Statement under penalty of perjury of good-faith belief of mistake

---

## 5. Repeat Infringers

FCA may terminate accounts of repeat infringers in appropriate circumstances.

---

## 6. Misrepresentation

Anyone who knowingly misrepresents infringement or counter-notification may be liable for damages under 17 U.S.C. § 512(f).
