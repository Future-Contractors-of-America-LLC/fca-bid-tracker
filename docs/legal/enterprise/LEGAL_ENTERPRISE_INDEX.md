## Purpose

This folder contains the **canonical enterprise legal corpus** for FCA (Future Contractors of America) and Auricrux platform sales to mid-market and Fortune 100 buyers. Documents are structured to align with standard procurement checklists: security review, privacy/DPA, MSA, SLA, subprocessors, export compliance, and accessibility.

**Storage convention:** All enterprise customer-facing and procurement legal templates live here. IP filing prep remains in `docs/legal/` (parent). Published web pages are derived from these sources.

1. **Counsel review** � Attorney reviews corpus; records redlines in version history.
