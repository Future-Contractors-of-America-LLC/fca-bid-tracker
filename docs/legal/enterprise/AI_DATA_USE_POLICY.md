# AI Data Use Policy

**Future Contractors of America LLC**  
**Version:** 1.0  
**Effective Date:** June 19, 2026  

> **Prepared for legal review — not legal advice.** Publish at https://futurecontractorsofamerica.com/ai-policy or incorporate in Terms.

---

## 1. Purpose

This policy describes how FCA uses artificial intelligence and machine learning in the Services, including Auricrux-guided workflows and Azure OpenAI integrations.

---

## 2. Principles

1. **Customer control:** Customer Data is processed to serve Customer's workspace, not to build public models for unrelated customers unless Customer opts in.
2. **Transparency:** AI-assisted outputs are identified where practicable.
3. **Human responsibility:** AI outputs require human review for bids, safety, compliance, and contractual commitments.
4. **Security:** AI prompts and outputs are protected per the [Security Addendum](./SECURITY_ADDENDUM.md).
5. **Compliance:** AI features comply with applicable law and Customer's DPA.

---

## 3. What We Do

| Activity | Description |
|----------|-------------|
| **Inference** | Process prompts and context to generate suggestions, summaries, and workflow assistance |
| **Logging** | Retain prompts/outputs for debugging, abuse prevention, and quality — per retention schedule |
| **Improvement** | Use aggregated, de-identified usage metrics to improve features |
| **Opt-in training** | Customer may opt in to allow anonymized patterns for product improvement — default **off** for enterprise |

---

## 4. What We Do Not Do

- Sell Customer Data to AI vendors for their model training
- Use Customer Data to train foundation models offered to other customers **without opt-in**
- Represent AI outputs as licensed professional engineering, legal, or safety certifications
- Deploy AI for fully automated binding contractual decisions without human approval

---

## 5. Subprocessors

AI inference may use **Microsoft Azure OpenAI / AI Foundry** per the [Subprocessor List](./SUBPROCESSOR_LIST.md). Enterprise customers may request zero-retention or regional deployment options during procurement.

---

## 6. Customer Obligations

- Review AI outputs before reliance in bids, estimates, and safety-critical decisions
- Do not submit PHI to non-HIPAA-configured Services
- Configure role-based access to AI features appropriately

---

## 7. Enterprise Options

Order Forms may specify:

- AI features disabled
- Dedicated Azure OpenAI deployment
- Zero data retention with subprocessor (where available)
- Prohibition on specific data categories in prompts

Contact legal@futurecontractorsofamerica.com.
