## 1. Purpose

This Acceptable Use Policy ("**AUP**") defines permitted and prohibited uses of FCA Services. Violations may result in suspension or termination under the Terms of Service.

---

## 2. Permitted Use

You may use the Services only for lawful internal business purposes related to construction, contracting, project management, training, and operations consistent with your subscription.

---

## 3. Prohibited Conduct

You will not, and will not permit users to:

### 3.1 Security and Integrity

- Probe, scan, or test vulnerabilities without written authorization
- Bypass authentication, access controls, or rate limits
- Introduce malware, viruses, or harmful code
- Interfere with Service availability (DDoS, excessive automated requests)
- Access another customer's workspace without authorization

### 3.2 Unlawful and Harmful Content

- Upload or transmit unlawful, defamatory, harassing, or discriminatory content
- Infringe intellectual property, privacy, or publicity rights
- Store or transmit child sexual abuse material (CSAM) Ś zero tolerance; reported to authorities
- Use the Services for fraudulent bids, false licensing claims, or misrepresentation to owners or regulators

### 3.3 Abuse of Platform

- Send spam or unsolicited bulk communications through the Services
- Resell or white-label the Services without agreement
- Use automated means to scrape the Services except via documented APIs within limits
- Misrepresent affiliation with FCA or Auricrux

### 3.4 AI and Automation Misuse

- Use AI features to generate false safety certifications, code compliance attestations, or licensed work product without qualified human review
- Attempt to extract model weights, system prompts, or proprietary Auricrux logic at scale
- Use outputs in violation of the [AI Data Use Policy](./AI_DATA_USE_POLICY.md)

### 3.5 Export and Sanctions

- Use the Services in embargoed countries or for sanctioned persons or entities
- See [Export Compliance Policy](./EXPORT_COMPLIANCE_AND_SANCTIONS_POLICY.md)
