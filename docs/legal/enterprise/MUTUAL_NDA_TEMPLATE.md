## PARTIES

**Future Contractors of America LLC** ("**FCA**"), and **[Counterparty legal name]** ("**Recipient**"). Each a "**Party**"; collectively "**Parties**."

**Effective Date:** [Date]

---

## 1. PURPOSE

The Parties wish to explore a business relationship regarding FCA contractor software and related services ("**Purpose**") and may disclose confidential information.

---

## 2. CONFIDENTIAL INFORMATION

"**Confidential Information**" means non-public information disclosed by a Party, marked confidential or reasonably understood to be confidential, including product roadmaps, pricing, technical data, security information, and business plans.

**Exclusions:** Public information, prior possession without restriction, independent development, or rightful third-party receipt.

---

## 3. OBLIGATIONS

Recipient will:

- Use Confidential Information only for the Purpose
- Protect with reasonable care (not less than own confidential information)
- Limit disclosure to employees and advisors with need-to-know under confidentiality obligations
- Not reverse engineer or disclose to competitors

---

## 4. COMPELLED DISCLOSURE

If compelled by law, Recipient gives prompt notice (if permitted) and cooperates on protective order.

---

## 5. NO LICENSE

No license or obligation to proceed with transaction is granted.

---

## 6. TERM

Confidentiality obligations survive **three (3) years** from disclosure; trade secrets survive as long as protected.

---

## 7. RETURN

Upon request, return or destroy Confidential Information; retain one archival copy if required by law.

---

## 8. REMEDIES

Injunctive relief available for breach without bond requirement where permitted.

---

## 9. GOVERNING LAW

Commonwealth of **Virginia**. Venue: Virginia courts.
