## 1. Introduction

Future Contractors of America LLC ("**FCA**," "**we**," "**us**," or "**our**") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect personal information when you use our website, platform, customer portal, mobile applications, academy, APIs, and related services (collectively, the "**Services**").

This policy applies to business customers, individual users, website visitors, and prospects. Enterprise customers may also be subject to a [Data Processing Agreement](./DATA_PROCESSING_AGREEMENT.md) ("**DPA**"); where a DPA conflicts with this policy for Customer Personal Data processed on behalf of a customer, the DPA controls.

**Contact:** privacy@futurecontractorsofamerica.com

## 3. Information We Collect

### 3.1 Information You Provide

- **Account data:** Name, email, phone, job title, company name, password (hashed), billing address
- **Organization data:** Company profile, licenses, team structure, role assignments
- **Workspace content:** Project data, bids, documents, communications, files you upload
- **Support data:** Messages, tickets, call recordings (if disclosed and consented)
- **Payment data:** Processed by Stripe; we receive limited billing metadata, not full card numbers

### 3.2 Information Collected Automatically

- **Usage data:** Feature usage, logins, clicks, session duration, error logs
- **Device and technical data:** IP address, browser type, operating system, device identifiers, time zone
- **Cookies and similar technologies:** See [Cookie Policy](./COOKIE_POLICY.md)

### 3.3 Information from Third Parties

- Authentication providers (e.g., Microsoft Entra ID / SSO)
- Payment processor (Stripe)
- Integration partners you authorize (e.g., project management tools)
- Public business registries and marketing sources (prospects only)

### 3.4 Sensitive Data

FCA does not intentionally collect sensitive categories (health, biometric, children's data under 13) through core contractor Services. **Calyndra** (AAC product line) has separate privacy terms at the Calyndra application. Do not upload sensitive personal data to FCA contractor Services unless required for your business and permitted by applicable law and your DPA.

## 4. How We Use Information

We use personal information to:

1. Provide, operate, and maintain the Services
2. Authenticate users and enforce security
3. Process payments and manage subscriptions
4. Deliver academy content and customer support
5. Send service, security, and transactional communications
6. Improve products, fix bugs, and develop features
7. Comply with legal obligations and respond to lawful requests
8. Detect fraud, abuse, and security incidents
9. Market FCA services to prospects (with opt-out where required)

**We do not sell personal information** as defined under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA/CPRA).

We do not use Customer Data to train general-purpose AI models for other customers unless you opt in or as described in the [AI Data Use Policy](./AI_DATA_USE_POLICY.md).

## 8. Retention

We retain personal information as long as needed to provide the Services, comply with law, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements.

| Category | Typical retention |
|----------|-------------------|
| Active account data | Duration of subscription + export window |
| Billing records | 7 years (tax/accounting) |
| Security logs | 12–24 months |
| Support tickets | 3 years after closure |
| Marketing suppression list | Until opt-in restored or legal limit |

Customer administrators may request earlier deletion subject to legal and contractual limits.

## 10. Your Rights and Choices

### 10.1 All Users

- Access, correct, or update account information in your profile
- Opt out of marketing emails via unsubscribe link
- Manage cookie preferences per [Cookie Policy](./COOKIE_POLICY.md)

### 10.2 EEA/UK (GDPR)

You may have rights to access, rectify, erase, restrict, port, and object to processing. Contact privacy@futurecontractorsofamerica.com. You may lodge a complaint with your supervisory authority.

### 10.3 California (CCPA/CPRA)

California residents may request to know, delete, and correct personal information, and opt out of "sale" or "sharing" (we do not sell). Authorized agent requests accepted with verification. Non-discrimination for exercising rights.

### 10.4 Enterprise Customers

End-user rights requests for data processed on your behalf should be directed through your organization administrator or per your DPA.

**Response timeline:** We respond within applicable legal timeframes (typically 30–45 days).
